Welcome to EARS-CONN 2026

DIGITAL EARTH AS INFRASTRUCTURE
FOR ETHICAL EMBODIED-AI

EARS-CONN 2026 is an initiative event on challenges and opportunities for Digital Earth—enabling ethical, sustainable, and safe embodied-AI in our lives. From AI agents on computers making decisions to robots serving on the ground, we explore how they contribute to building, supporting, and protecting Digital Earth—where cloud, connectivity, data, compute, and AI/ML models play central roles.

Data from satellites, autonomous cars, robots, airplanes, drones, and UAVs keeps Digital Earth updated for real-time ethical decisions. Governance and regulation are the backbone for keeping these decisions ethical and safe. We examine these challenges from three angles: Technical (research & industry), Regulation (standards & governance), and Assurance (insurance & monitoring).

Join us for 2 days of invited speakers, panel discussions, a hands-on workshop on building a digital twin of Earth, poster presentations, and a challenge for Digital Earth ideas focused on urban and environmental sustainability and safety.

Digital Earth as Infrastructure

A Smarter NYC — Robotics, AI & Digital Earth Transforming Urban Life

Digital Earth is the intelligent infrastructure that enables ethical embodied-AI. Cloud, connectivity, data, compute, and AI/ML models power this vision. Data flows from satellites, autonomous cars, robots, airplanes, drones, and UAVs—building and keeping Digital Earth updated for real-time ethical decisions.

Satellite & Remote Sensing

Global environmental monitoring, Earth observation, and real-time mapping from space. Satellite data informs digital twins, urban planning, and ethical decision-making.

Connectivity & Compute

Cloud infrastructure, networks, and compute enable data fusion across scales—from orbital sensing to ground-level robot perception for autonomous systems.

Robots, Drones & Autonomous Systems

Ground robots, autonomous vehicles, drones, and UAVs collect and contribute data to build and maintain the digital twin of Earth for sustainable, safe operations.

Together, these layers form a continuous sensing fabric that supports Digital Earth—enabling ethical, sustainable, and safe embodied-AI from AI agents in the cloud to robots on the ground.

Challenges & Opportunities: Three Angles

We examine Digital Earth for ethical embodied-AI from three essential angles—each critical for safe, sustainable, and responsible deployment:

1. Technical

Research and industry: scalable robotics, embodied AI, remote sensing, satellite data, cloud, connectivity, compute, and AI/ML models. How technology builds and maintains Digital Earth for real-time ethical decisions.

2. Regulation

Standards and governance: safety certification, environmental compliance, policy frameworks. Governance and regulation are the backbone for keeping embodied-AI decisions ethical and safe.

3. Assurance

Insurance and monitoring: accountability, risk management, and assurance for autonomous systems. How we verify, monitor, and ensure ethical and safe deployment of embodied-AI.

Invited speakers and panelists from across these angles—research, industry, policy, standards, and assurance—come together to tell the Digital Earth story and address challenges for ethical, sustainable, and safe embodied-AI in our lives.
