Embodied-AI Meets Digital-Earth

Listening from Space, Acting on the Ground — Sustainability Assurance for Resilient Cities
New York, NY, USA — September 15–18, 2026

Embodied-AI for Resiliency and Sustainability (EARS-CONN)

Earth can be defined as a closed-loop, connected physical system where natural phenomena — some of them disastrous — occur. Some are completely beyond human control yet inevitable. Human impact can directly mitigate or aggravate the effects of catastrophic phenomena such as flood, earthquake, drought, and more. In this conference we aim to use state-of-the-art technologies — embodied-AI, robotics, remote sensing, and connectivity — to build an intelligent digital-twin of the Earth system. That foundation will enhance both our proactive and reactive development of solutions for a resilient and sustainable urban ecosystem.

Integrating Remote Sensing and Embodied-AI Robotics to Build the Digital-Earth Infrastructure for Metropolitan Sustainability

Satellite & Remote Sensing

Satellite data feeds Digital-Earth for real-time urban mapping and sustainability scenarios — flooding, climate risk, wildfire impact, and infrastructure resilience.

Robots, Drones, & Autonomous Vehicles

Embodied-AI built into robots, drones, autonomous vehicles and urban IoT devices feed ground-level data into Digital-Earth — enabling real-time sustainability awareness, assurance, and emergency response through 5G/6G connectivity.
