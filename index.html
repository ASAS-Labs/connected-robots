Conference Vision

As autonomous robotic systems begin operating at large scale across cities, infrastructure, and environmental domains, EARS-CONN brings together experts from robotics & embodied AI, environmental science, remote sensing, satellite/CubeSat systems, policy & governance, and urban systems & sustainability.

Central question: How do we design safe, scalable, ethical, and environmentally responsible embodied AI systems for a planet where robots operate among humans and ecosystems?
