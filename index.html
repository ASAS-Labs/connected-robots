DIGITAL-EARTH for ETHICAL EMBODIED-AI

EARS-CONN 2026 is a long-vision initiative on improving life in the NYC area with Robotics and AI — Digital-Earth as Infrastructure. We are at a pivotal moment: robotics and AI are advancing fast—from cloud agents to autonomous robots, drones, and vehicles—while Digital-Earth (real-time environmental and urban data) is emerging but still fragmented. Cities like NYC face climate, mobility, safety, and equity challenges. Data from satellites, autonomous cars, robots, airplanes, drones, and UAVs keeps Digital-Earth updated for real-time ethical decisions. Governance and regulation are the backbone for keeping these decisions ethical and safe. We examine challenges from three angles: Technical (research & industry), Regulation (standards & governance), and Assurance (insurance & monitoring).

Satellite & Remote Sensing

Global environmental monitoring, Earth observation, and real-time mapping from space. Data from satellites flows into Digital-Earth—the intelligent infrastructure for ethical embodied-AI—building and keeping it updated for real-time ethical decisions.

Connectivity, Compute & Storage

Cloud, connectivity, data, compute, storage, and AI/ML models power Digital-Earth. Telecommunications and networks enable data fusion across scales—from orbital sensing to ground-level robot perception and IoT for autonomous systems.

Robots, Drones, IoT & Autonomous Systems

Data flows from ground robots, autonomous cars, drones, UAVs, airplanes, and IoT sensors—building and keeping Digital-Earth updated for real-time ethical decisions and sustainable, safe operations.
