Ethical Embodied-AI Workshop Series: Safe and Responsible Decisions
New York, New Yrok, USA — June 14–18, 2026 (tentative) — 2 days (talks + panels) — 2 days (workshops + innovation challenge)

Conference Vision

  • Responsible embodied AI at scale: design, govern, and deploy autonomous robots across cities, infrastructure, and natural environments to serve society safely, sustainably, and responsibly.
  • A global multidisciplinary platform: unite robotics and AI with environmental science, remote sensing, satellite/CubeSat engineering, policy, and urban innovation to address technical, ethical, and environmental challenges of large-scale deployment.
  • Sustainability-aligned impact: inspire safe AI design principles that minimize ecological impact, reduce resource consumption, and strengthen resilience through collaborative dialogue, hands-on innovation, and policy-informed discussion.
Tentative dates: June 14–18, 2026
