Call for Innovation Challenge Submissions

Submit ideas and compete in pitch competitions: how fused robot + remote-sensing information can help different industries build resilient, sustainable urban ecosystems in NYC.

NYC Innovation Challenge

We invite submissions for the EARS-CONN 2026 Innovation Challenge. Proposals should show how fused information—combining robot data with global remote sensing, satellite data, and connectivity—can serve different industries and build resilient, sustainable urban ecosystems in New York City.

Industry Applications — How Fused Information Helps

Pitch how fused robot + remote-sensing data can serve industries such as:

  • Parametric insurance — Trigger-based coverage using real-time environmental and robotic data
  • Basis risk — Reducing mismatch between indexed triggers and actual losses
  • Environmental monitoring and risk assessment
  • Urban infrastructure inspection and maintenance
  • Disaster response, prevention, and prediction

Resources & Infrastructure

Participants can leverage:

  • Existing urban infrastructure and connectivity
  • Satellite and Earth observation data
  • Robotic platforms and embodied AI solutions
  • Open datasets and APIs for environmental sensing

Submission & Pitch Competition

Shortlisted teams will present their ideas in live pitch sessions. A jury panel will evaluate proposals and award prizes for the most impactful innovations.
