About EARS-CONN

EARS-CONN 2026 (New York City) is a single-track, multidisciplinary forum on Ethical Embodied-AI Workshop Series: Safe and Responsible Decisions. The conference connects robotics and embodied AI with environmental science, remote sensing, satellite/CubeSat systems, policy & governance, and urban systems & sustainability.

Central question: How do we design safe, scalable, ethical, and environmentally responsible embodied AI systems for a planet where robots operate among humans and ecosystems?
