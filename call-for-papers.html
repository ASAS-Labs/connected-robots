Call for Poster Presentations

Submit an abstract or short paper for poster presentation at EARS-CONN 2026. We welcome contributions on Digital Earth, embodied-AI, ethical/sustainable/safe systems, and related topics.

Scope

EARS-CONN 2026 is an initiative event on Digital Earth as infrastructure for ethical embodied-AI. We seek poster presentations that align with our theme: from AI agents on computers to robots on the ground, how they contribute to building, supporting, and protecting Digital Earth—where cloud, connectivity, data, compute, and AI/ML models play central roles.

Topics of Interest

  • Digital Earth: infrastructure, data fusion, digital twins
  • Embodied-AI for ethical, sustainable, and safe deployment
  • Data from satellites, robots, drones, autonomous vehicles, UAVs
  • Technical, regulation, or assurance perspectives
  • Urban and environmental sustainability and safety
  • Governance, standards, and monitoring for ethical decisions

Submission Format

Submit an abstract (1–2 pages) or short paper (2–4 pages) for poster presentation. Accepted submissions will be presented in the poster session during the 2-day event.
