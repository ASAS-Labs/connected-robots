Embodied-AI meets Digital-Earth for Sustainability Assurance of Connected Cities (EARS-CONN)

Call for Poster Presentations

EARS-CONN is Embodied-AI for Resiliency and Sustainability. Submit an abstract or short paper for poster presentation at EARS-CONN 2026. Peer reviewed. We welcome contributions aligned with embodied-AI, Digital-Earth, and sustainability assurance for connected cities.

Scope

EARS-CONN 2026 is a long-vision initiative on Embodied-AI meets Digital-Earth for sustainability assurance of connected cities. We seek poster presentations that address implementation plans—from embodied-AI agents to robots on the ground—how they build, support, and protect Digital-Earth through cloud, connectivity, remote sensing, and AI/ML for resilient and sustainable urban ecosystems.

Topics of Interest

  • Improving NYC life: robotics and AI for urban quality of life, mobility, safety, sustainability
  • Implementation plans: Digital-Earth and embodied-AI for sustainability assurance
  • Digital-Earth: infrastructure, data fusion, Digital-Earth for urban and environmental applications
  • Data from satellites, robots, drones, autonomous vehicles, UAVs—building and maintaining Digital-Earth
  • Technical, regulation, or assurance perspectives for NYC and urban deployment
  • Urban and environmental sustainability and safety in the NYC area
  • Governance, standards, and monitoring for ethical decisions

Submission Format

Submit an abstract (1–2 pages) or short paper (2–4 pages) for poster presentation. Accepted submissions will be presented in the poster session during the 2-day event.
