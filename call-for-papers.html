Call for Papers

Submit methods to build models from global and local data—fusing robot and remote-sensing information for sustainable urban ecosystems, with a focus on NYC.

Special Issues

EARS-CONN 2026 invites submissions for possible special issues on methods to build models from global and local data. We seek papers that combine the robot point of view with the global view from remote sensors—satellite data, Earth observation, and local robotic sensing—to serve connected robots that build more sustainable urban ecosystems.

Topics of Interest

  • Methods for fusing global (remote sensing, satellite) and local (robot) data
  • Model-building approaches for urban ecosystem monitoring and resilience
  • Connected robots for environment-aware decision-making
  • Robotic systems for environmental response, prevention, and prediction
  • Ethical and sustainable design of embodied AI for cities
