Hands-On Workshop: Building a Digital Twin of Earth

Learn how to use data from robots to satellites to build the digital twin of Earth. This hands-on workshop guides participants through integrating multi-source data for Digital Earth applications.

Workshop Overview

Digital Earth relies on data from many sources—satellites, autonomous cars, robots, airplanes, drones, and UAVs. This workshop provides hands-on experience in fusing and using these data to build and maintain a digital twin of Earth for real-time ethical decisions.

What You Will Learn

  • Data sources: satellite imagery, robot sensing, drone/UAV data, environmental sensors
  • Data fusion and integration for digital twin construction
  • Cloud, connectivity, compute, and AI/ML in the Digital Earth stack
  • Practical tools and workflows for urban and environmental applications
