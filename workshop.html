Workshop: Robotics for Space & Earth

Harness the power of regional partnerships to increase and accelerate access to the aerospace industry's Skilled Technical Workforce (STW). This workshop focuses on developing job-ready talent aligned with NASA suppliers and Artemis mission needs.

Robotics on Space or Earth

Explore robotics applications across the full spectrum—from terrestrial and environmental monitoring to space exploration and lunar operations. Topics include autonomous systems for Earth observation, robotic platforms for Artemis missions, and the convergence of space and Earth robotics for sustainable, connected ecosystems.

Regional Partnerships & Skilled Technical Workforce

Harness the power of regional partnerships to increase and accelerate access to the aerospace industry's Skilled Technical Workforce (STW). Establish state and regional hubs to develop job-ready Skilled Technical Workers through non-bachelor's pathways—aligned with NASA suppliers and Artemis mission needs.

This initiative connects academia, industry, and government to build a pipeline of technical talent for the aerospace sector.
