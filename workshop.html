Embodied-AI meets Digital-Earth for Sustainability Assurance of Connected Cities (EARS-CONN)

Hands-On Workshop: Digital-Earth Builders

EARS-CONN is Embodied-AI for Resiliency and Sustainability. Learn how to use data from robots to satellites to build Digital-Earth. This hands-on workshop supports our initiative for resilient and sustainable connected cities through embodied-AI, robotics, and Digital-Earth.

Workshop Overview

Digital-Earth relies on data from many sources—satellites, embodied-AI systems, robots, drones, and sensors. This workshop provides hands-on experience in fusing and using these data to build and maintain Digital-Earth for real-time ethical decisions—supporting sustainability assurance for resilient and sustainable urban ecosystems.

What You Will Learn

  • Data sources: satellite imagery, robot sensing, drone/UAV data, environmental sensors
  • Data fusion and integration for Digital-Earth construction
  • Cloud, connectivity, compute, and AI/ML in the Digital-Earth stack
  • Practical tools and workflows for urban and environmental applications—including NYC use cases
