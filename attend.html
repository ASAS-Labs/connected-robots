Attend EARS-CONN 2026

EARS-CONN is Embodied-AI for Resiliency and Sustainability. We use state-of-the-art technologies — embodied-AI, robotics, remote sensing, and connectivity — to build an intelligent digital-twin of the Earth system for a resilient and sustainable urban ecosystem. Register for this initiative: keynotes, panels, hands-on workshop on building Digital-Earth, poster session, and the Digital-Earth Challenge.

Talks & Panels — Three Angles

We invite speakers and panelists from across three angles to tell the Digital-Earth story:

  • Technical — Research and industry: how technology builds and maintains Digital-Earth
  • Regulation — Standards and governance: keeping embodied-AI decisions ethical and safe
  • Assurance — Insurance and monitoring: verifying and ensuring safe deployment

From embodied-AI agents to robots on the ground—how they build, support, and protect Digital-Earth for ethical, resilient, and sustainable deployment.

Hands-On Workshop — Digital-Earth Builders

Topics covered in the workshop:

  • Data fusion from satellites, robots, drones, and sensors
  • Cloud, connectivity, compute, and AI/ML for Digital-Earth
  • Practical workflows for urban and environmental applications
