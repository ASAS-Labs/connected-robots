Embodied-AI meets Digital-Earth for Sustainability Assurance of Connected Cities (EARS-CONN)

Attend EARS-CONN 2026

EARS-CONN brings together the technologies that build the Digital-Earth — from ground-level autonomous robots and drones to satellite remote sensing — and the communities that need it most: city planners, infrastructure engineers, insurers, and first responders. Join keynotes, panels, and hands-on workshops exploring how embodied-AI drives smarter city planning, more resilient urban infrastructure, and a new generation of proactive insurance intelligence for climate-exposed cities.

Conference Registration

Register for EARS-CONN 2026 to attend the full program. Registration includes access to all keynote talks, multidisciplinary panels, hands-on workshop, poster session, and challenge presentations — all focused on embodied-AI, Digital-Earth, sustainability assurance, and urban insurance intelligence for connected cities.

A dedicated track explores how Digital-Earth shifts insurance from reactive to proactive: satellite and aerial imagery combined with ground-level robotic data construct property-specific digital twins that support hyper-local risk scoring, personalized premiums, faster claims, and ethical automated decision-making. Remote sensing alone cannot resolve the detail needed in dense urban environments — roof conditions, façade damage, drainage micro-topography — making embodied-AI robotics an essential complement for accurate risk assessment.
