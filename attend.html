Talks & Panels — Connecting the Dots

Main value proposition: We invite speakers and panelists from across the Ethical Embodied-AI triangle to address connecting the dots as a multidisciplinary problem. Different stakeholders come together:

  • AI safety researchers
  • Environment and sustainability experts
  • Robotics researchers
  • Remote sensing and satellite data specialists
  • Policy, regulation, and industry

Together they explore how the robot point of view and global remote-sensing view can combine to build more sustainable urban ecosystems, with a focus on NYC needs.
